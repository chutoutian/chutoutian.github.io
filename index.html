About Me

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Klara Nahrstedt at the Multimedia Operating Systems and Networking (MONET) group.

My current research interests are 1) multimedia system and networking with applications of adaptive video streaming, cloud VR gaming, and live 360 video; 2) underwater communication via mobile devices. Previously, I did some research in wireless communication with a focus on multiple access techniques.

I enjoy water activities including swimming, SCUBA diving, and surfing. I am a huge fan of animes.
